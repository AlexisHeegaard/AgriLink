Frequently Asked Questions

What are "Class 2" products?

Fresh fruits and vegetables with minor cosmetic defects that retailers often reject[cite: 98, 101]. We rescue them to support farmers and lower your costs[cite: 110].

How does delivery work?

We aggregate orders at your office hub to eliminate carbon-heavy home deliveries[cite: 97, 134]. You retrieve your basket via a secure access code[cite: 164].
